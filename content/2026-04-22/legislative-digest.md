## 💡 EDITORIAL NOTE

Two threads are converging in Q2 2026: the GPAI Code of Practice is crystallising at the EU level while member states rush to designate their national supervisory authorities ahead of the August deadline under Article 70. Expect the CoP's systemic-risk language and national competent authority mandates to collide — in particular on who signs off on pre-deployment evaluation reports. Worth tracking whether any member state tries to unilaterally tighten CoP commitments via supervisory-authority guidance.
